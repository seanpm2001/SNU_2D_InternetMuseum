SocialNetworkUltimate (SNU) legends timeline

2018

? date ? - SocialNetworkUltimate starts in SNU 1.0, as a template only in GSites, which will later stop receiving updates due to issues with Google

2019

January 10th 2019 - Planning for the new SNU begins in JavaScript (bad syntax, and non-compilable) which later inspires SNU 3.0.x

January 30th 2019 - After accidentally and successfully linking one file to another, my interest in HTML skyrocketed, and SNU 3.x began development at a rapid pace.

March 12th 2019 - A stable, Google-free SNU is now built, and is far more advanced than version 1.0

June 14th 2019 - First release of SNU 3.0.x with 6 month support, SNU 3.05 is released privately to Sean

July of 2019 - Update log undergoes massive renovation, and becomes most advanced page on SNU, and as the test subject for most new changes
